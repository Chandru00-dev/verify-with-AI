Pro Tips for Accuracy

How to prompt the AI for the best possible analysis results.

Be Specific

Instead of "Analyze this," try "Check the health claims mentioned in the first 5 minutes of this video."

Request Sources

Always ask for citations: "Provide the primary research papers or news articles that support your conclusion."

Define Language

Get localized results by prompting in your language: "இந்த வீடியோவின் உண்மையை தமிழிலேயே ஆராயவும்."

Use Context

Mention what the file is: "This PDF is a political manifesto, verify the economic statistics mentioned on page 4."
